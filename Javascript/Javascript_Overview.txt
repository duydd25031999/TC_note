Brief Description
- This project is designed to help me practice and refine my spoken responses to JavaScript interview questions (covering ES6+, core language concepts, asynchronous patterns, data structures, and browser‑related APIs) in preparation for English‑only Senior‑level job interviews.  
- I am a Senior Front‑end Developer with leadership and presale experience, and I estimate my current English speaking proficiency to be at IELTS Speaking 5.5.  
- In this project, I will attach a series of Markdown files-each covering a different topic in JavaScript (e.g., language basics, ES6 syntax, functions, objects, arrays, promises). ChatGPT will assist me in expressing my answers clearly, accurately, and confidently in English.  
- The project’s focus is not just correctness, but also clarity, fluency, and suitability for a real interview setting.

Key Objectives
- Transform my existing JavaScript notes and practice questions into well‑structured, spoken‑style English responses.  
- Simulate answering JavaScript interview questions with a tone appropriate for a Senior developer role.  
- Receive actionable feedback and language suggestions that match my current level (IELTS Speaking 5.5) and help improve fluency.  
- Build a reliable JavaScript knowledge base that I can refer to and rehearse from during my preparation phase.  
- Develop confidence in delivering technical answers clearly in a 100 %‑English interview environment.

Problem Statement
- While I have solid technical knowledge and leadership experience in JavaScript development, I find it challenging to express this knowledge fluently and clearly in English under interview pressure. 
- Given that my speaking level is approximately IELTS 5.5, I often struggle with expressing complex thoughts in a structured and concise way during high‑stakes conversations.
- I need a way to systematically improve my delivery-especially for technical questions-by refining my spoken responses and getting English‑language feedback aligned with both my content and my communication style. 
- Traditional learning tools don’t offer real‑time, personalized coaching with my existing knowledge files-which is the gap this project will fill.

- Audience: This content is for myself as a Senior‑level Front‑end Developer targeting English‑speaking job interviews with a JavaScript emphasis.

- Supportive Output:

  - Notes on alternative expressions or sentence structures.
